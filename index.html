Our Story at Laurelle & Co.

Laurelle & Co. was born from a deep-seated passion for exceptional coffee and the community it brings together. It first opened on March 15, 2021, introducing the art and flavor of high-quality craft coffee to the vibrant neighborhood of Barangay Holy Spirit, Quezon City.

Since its opening, Laurelle & Co. has welcomed countless customers, earning a reputation as a beloved neighborhood hub. This success is a testament to its commitment to both its craft and its community.

The brand is now partnered with a renowned franchising consultant, with plans to soon bring the unique Laurelle & Co. experience nationwide.

Come visit Laurelle & Co. — where every sip, bite, and smile is made with heart. 💛
